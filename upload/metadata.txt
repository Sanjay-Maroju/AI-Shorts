TITLE:
WARNING: Avoid Public USB Chargers (Juice Jacking)

DESCRIPTION:
Public USB charging stations are a major security risk! Learn about "juice jacking" and how criminals can steal your personal data, passwords, and banking details. Protect your privacy by always using your own wall charger or a power bank.
